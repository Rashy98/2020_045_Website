Business Solution

About

An IoT Based intelligent chamber that is used to identify potential COVID-19 suspects by taking the aforementioned symptoms into account. Once a person enters the chamber, our solution uses Neural Networks and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to identify COVID-19 symptoms such as Fever, Anosmia, Cough, and Shortness of Breath.

The product is designed and developed under four main components.

  • Cough Analysis
  • Body Temperature
  • Shortness of Breath Detection
  • Anosmia Condition Detection

Body Temperature

The basic purpose of fever detection is to take a non-contact temperature reading of the body. In this component, a thermal camera is utilized to measure the body temperature.

Cough Analysis

This component uses two Neural Networks to predict if the force cough the person inside the chamber is a COVID-19 positive cough or not. One Neural network is used to determine whether a cough is present in the captured audio and the other is used to predict if the acquired cough is a COVID-19 positive cough or not.

Shortness of Breath Detection

Using thermal image processing, the device monitors the individual's respiration rate. As a result, a thermal camera is used in this system to measure the rate of respiration.

Anosmia Condition Detection

When the person enters the chamber, the system will ask a few questions about the odor. The collected results will be compared to the saturation level to determine whether or not the individual is a COVID-19 suspect.

Person Authentication

Once someone walks into the chamber, the system detects them and starts the recognition process. The technology includes two approaches to identify the person. Face and voice recognition systems identify individuals and authorize the testing process to begin.

Report Generation

A report is a very useful tool for management to utilize on a regular basis to track and analyze each employee/student. The person's name, identification number, and the daily results of the testing method are all included in the report.

Why Incov Chamber?

The Incov chamber is a smart chamber which can detect the possible COVID-19 infected individuals. So far, the technique of measuring the body temperature is used to identify the possible individuals with virus infected. But in the Incov chamber, 3 other symptoms are used to check along with measuring the body temperature to make it more efficient in finding those persons. As the chamber is fully automated, there is no need of third person to carry out the process, which make it more safer.

Other than checking for the symptoms daily, the Incov chamber maintains records of the test results, allowing your company to use them in future. Also, these records are used to predict if a particular person may get infected with the corona virus.
